10. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

Q: What documents do I need to buy a car?

A: Aadhaar card, PAN card, 2 passport photos, address proof, and income proof (salary slips / ITR) for finance. Cash buyers need only Aadhaar and PAN.

Q: How long does delivery take after booking?

A: In-stock vehicles: 2–5 days. Out-of-stock/custom colour: 4–8 weeks depending on variant. We will inform you of the exact date at the time of booking.

Q: Can I negotiate the price?

A: Prices are as per manufacturer guidelines. However, we offer the best combination of cash discount + exchange bonus + accessories. Speak to our sales executive for the best deal.

Q: Is extended warranty available?

A: Yes. We offer 1-year and 2-year extended warranty plans. Hyundai standard warranty: 3 years / 1,00,000 km. Tata: 3 years/1,25,000 km. Maruti: 2 years/40,000 km.

Q: Do you offer home delivery?

A: Yes, home delivery available within Hyderabad limits at no extra charge. Outside city: ₹2,000–5,000 transport charges apply.

Q: Can I cancel my booking?

A: Bookings can be cancelled within 7 days. Booking amount (₹11,000–₹25,000) is refunded within 10 working days. After 7 days, cancellation charges may apply.

Q: Do you buy old cars without selling a new one?

A: Yes. We have a dedicated used car team. We evaluate and buy your car at the best market price even if you don't purchase from us.

Q: What is the booking amount?

A: ₹11,000 for hatchbacks/sedans, ₹21,000 for SUVs, ₹31,000 for premium/EV models.

Q: How do I check delivery status?

A: Call our showroom or WhatsApp us at +91 98765 43210 with your booking reference number.
